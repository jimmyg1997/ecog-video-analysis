IEEE-SMC-2025 Competition

Competition Details
  • Event: IEEE-SMC-2025 ECoG Video Analysis Competition
  • Location: Vienna, Austria (October 5-6, 2025)
  • Duration: BR41N.IO Hackathon
  • Objective: ECoG Video Watching Analysis
Our Approach
  • Advanced Preprocessing: Multi-stage signal processing pipeline
  • Multiple Algorithms: Template correlation, EEGNet, Transformer
  • Real-time Visualization: Interactive web application
  • Video Analysis: 7 different analysis types with annotations
